About Me

Hello I am Eduardo. I am originally from São Paulo, Brazil, and currently based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. I'm an incoming sophomore at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign studying Electrical Engineering.

This summer, I am interning at Berco of America, a thyssenkrupp subsidiary in Waukesha, WI, where I work with the process engineering and quality teams on manufacturing operations.

Professionally, I'm drawn to high-stakes hardware problems where the boundaries of what's possible are constantly being pushed. I'm especially interested in healthcare technology, robotics and automation, and the intersection of silicon and photonics. I'm still early in defining the exact path I want to pursue, but I'm motivated by opportunities to build impactful, real-world systems.

Outside of engineering, I mentor an FTC robotics team, play various musical instruments, and enjoy playing pickleball, golf, and pick-up soccer with friends. I am also currently training for my first half marathon.
